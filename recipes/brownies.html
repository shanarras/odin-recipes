Mochi Brownies

I love mochi. Whoever thought of incorporating mochiko flour into baked goods was a genius. Chewy goodness.

Ingredients

  • 130g dark brown sugar
  • 169g Mochiko flour
  • 4g(1/4 tsp) baking powder
  • 15g cocoa powder
  • 1.5g (1/4 tsp) salt
  • 1g (1/2 tsp) instant coffee
  • 30g unsalted butter melted
  • 200ml whole milk
  • 1 large egg
  • 55g dark chocolate chips

Steps

  1. Weigh and sift all brown sugar, flour, baking powder, cocoa powder, salt and instant coffee into a medium bowl. Whisk to combine well, then set aside.
  2. Preheat oven to 350°F or 180°C. Grease the muffin tray with melted butter and set aside.
  3. Pour the remaining butter into a medium bowl along with milk and egg. Whisk to combine well.
  4. Add in the dry ingredients and whisk again to combine.
  5. Pour and divide mixture evenly into 12 muffin holes. Top each with chocolate chips.
  6. Bake for 40 minutes or until top crust is fully formed but slightly springy to the touch.
  7. Chill them on cooling rack for 10 minutes. Using a toothpick, push each out from the side.
